NTST Series

MODEL NTSTS

NEELTARA LED Model STS is based on SMD LEDS designed for street lights applications. These are high performance, maintenance free, long life luminaire. High power SMD LEDs from Japan with sophisticated, high performance lenses and reflectors of European origin ensures optimized light distribution to cover wide area.

Model NTS offers excellent saving on energy. MAW model is available in 50, 100, 130, 150, 200 and 250 watts. Other wattages can also be supplied.

Model NTSTC

NEELTARA LED Model STC is based on COB LEDS designed for street lights applications. These are high performance, maintenance free, long life luminaire. High power COB LEDs from Japan with sophisticated, high performance reflectors of European origin ensures optimized light distribution to cover wide area..

Model STC offers excellent saving on energy. STC model is available in 50, 70 and 140 watts. Other wattages can also be supplied.
