Features

High performance long life LEDS

NEELTARA LEDS are very long life and work for more than 50000 hrs. Very high lumen 140 lm per watt. Typical CCT 5500 to 6000. Other CCT lights can be supplied as required.

Tailor made light beam spread solutions

LEDs from top manufacturer from Japan with light spread angle of 120° whcih can be further tailored in the range of 3° to 270° with the help of sophisticated optical lenses from Finland .

High efficiency Driver

High efficiency integrated LED drivers with protection from over voltage, over current and high temperature.

All Aluminium fixture anodized to high industrial standards. IP 65 compliant fixtures are used for outdoor and industrial lighting applications.
