MODEL NTCLN

NEELTARA LED CLN Series lights are luminaires specifically designed for clean room area lighting applications. These dust free luminaires with minimal resistance to airflow, with glass face for easy and universal sanitization are suitable for highly hygenic facilities like hospitals, laboratories, pharmaceutical and food industries. Seamless silicon gaskets ensure very high degree of sealing between interfaces.

MODEL NTWAD

NEELTARA LED WAD FLP are designed for retrofitting conventional well glass fittings in industrial fixtures. LEDs from Japan with sophisticated, high performance polycarbonate diffuser sheet from Germany ensures optimized light distribution to cover wide area light distribution.
