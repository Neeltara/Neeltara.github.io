SERIES NTPOP

Model NTPOP

NEELTARA LED Model POP is used for recess and surface fitting. All aluminium fixture and high performance LEDs from Japan, reflectors from Germany and polycarbonate diffuser sheets from Germany are used for high level performance.

NEELTARA LED POP series lights are high performance, maintenance free luminaire specifically designed for recess fitting and indoor low roof area lighting. These are also available in surface mounted models. THese models are available in 12 watt, 22 watt and 45 watt.
